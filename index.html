Sports Analytics Club at UC Davis

Welcome to our page!
Currently under development, we are working to create a club that implements our passion for sports along with our knowledge of data analysis!

Visit us to know more!

Teams

There are 3 divisions that one may be apart of. Projects. Journalism. Business. Our mission is simple! To create dynamic research projects that see our favorite teams/players in new ways. Using real data, we aim to connect the CS and sports world in creative ways.

Projects

Our projects section will focus on creating numerous models generally focused on the NFL and NBA. This section will be run by the Department's Project Head, Amar Singh.

Journalism

The Journalism department will be focused mainly upon the models that we create. Incorporating their own beliefs within the sports world combined with the data will help provide a unique perspective.

Business

The Business team will primarily focus on gathering funds, managing the social media presence, and running events for the club. Eventually we will look to network and consult for teams in the future.
