Quarterback Comparison

An aesthetically pleasing radar chart catered
towards common quarterback stats. Each tier
represents how much better a quarterback was in
comparison to the best of the quarterbacks within
the NFL. The closer the line is to the outside, the
better they were.

NBA MVP Analysis

A machine learning algorithm that predicts the top
5 in the MVP race year by year. MVP factors are
calculated by a variety of stats that are determined
by the algorithm. The algorithm is ~87% accurate
using data from 1991 onwards















VOR Model

Uses pandas dataframes to calculate a value
based approach to determine the best order to
fantasy football drafting. Computes
which players to take by taking aprojected PPR
value and subtracting it by an average league
player's PPR value,thus yielding the most
valuable player to take.
